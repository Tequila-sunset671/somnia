# Notes and Graph

These notes are plain markdown files in a vault. Link them with [[double
brackets]] and Somnia builds a force-directed **graph** of the connections (open
Notes → Graph, or the mini-graph on the Home screen).

- Backlinks appear automatically
- Connect an external Obsidian vault (read-only) from Customize
- Click a node in the graph to open that note

See [[Welcome to Somnia]] and [[Spaces and Tabs]].
