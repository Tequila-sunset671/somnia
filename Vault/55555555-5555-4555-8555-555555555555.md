# Privacy

Somnia is local-first: your notes and history stay on your machine.

- **Tracker / ad blocking** — toggle in Customize (native content blocker)
- **Private tabs** — ⌘⇧N: no history, no disk cache, not saved to the session
- **Favicons** are fetched from the sites themselves, not a third-party service
