# Spaces and Tabs

Somnia groups tabs into **spaces** (the colored dots in the sidebar). Tabs load
lazily and sleep when idle, so a wall of tabs stays light on memory.

- ⌘T new tab · ⌘W close · ⌘1–9 jump to a tab
- Drag the sidebar edge to resize or collapse it
- Cmd-click a link → opens a background tab
